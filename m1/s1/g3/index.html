Focaccia with Caramelized Onions,
Cranberries and Brie

Wow your taste buds with this festive focaccia appetizer topped with sweet caramelized onions, tart cranberries, and cremy brie.It will become your new favorite holiday starter!

Fresh Cranberries Recipes That Go Beyound Souce

Ingredients

For the focaccia:

  • 4 cups (500g) all-purpose flour
  • 1 1/2 teaspoon instant yeast
  • 1 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 3/4 cups (350g) lukewarm water
  • 1/4 cup extra vergin olive oil, plus more for the pan

For the topping:

  • 3 medium yellow onions, thingly sliced
  • 1 tablespoon balsamic vinegar
  • 1-2 tablespoon olive oil, for brushing on top
  • 10 ounces brie,cut into cubes(scrape rind off with a paring knife if desired, though it's not necessary)
  • 1 1/2 cups fresh or frozen cranberries

Method

  1. Make the focaccia dough

In a medium-size mixing bowl, or in a stand mixer with a paddle attchment, mix toghether all the dough ingredients until the flour is fully absorbed. You don't need to actually knead the dough,though i sometimes knead it a few times in the bowl when mixing by hand to incorporate the last of the flour...
